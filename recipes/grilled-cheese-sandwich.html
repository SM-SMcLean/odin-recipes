Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Directions

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place cheese between the two slices of bread, buttered sides facing out.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Grill sandwich until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side.
