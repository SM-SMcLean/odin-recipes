Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (14 ounce) can chicken broth
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

  1. In a saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Saute onion and garlic in olive oil until onion is translucent. Stir in tomatoes, chicken broth, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, and then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. With a hand blender, puree the soup until smooth. Return to saucepan, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, and slowly stir in cream. Heat through, but do not boil. Serve with a garnish of fresh basil.
